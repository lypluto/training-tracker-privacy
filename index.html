Privacy Policy

Training Tracker

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Training Tracker is a mobile application that helps you log and track your workouts. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection and Storage

Training Tracker does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers. All workout data you enter in the app (such as body parts, exercises, and training records) is stored locally on your device only.

The app does not require you to create an account or sign in, and the developer does not have access to the workout data you enter.

Third-Party Services

Training Tracker does not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or other services that collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

Training Tracker is not specifically directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information within the app, you can remove the app and its local data from the device at any time.

Data Security

Because your workout data is stored only on your device, its security depends on how you secure your device (for example, with a passcode, biometrics, or other device-level protection). If you uninstall the app or reset your device, the locally stored data may be deleted and cannot be recovered by the developer.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. You should review this policy periodically for any changes.
